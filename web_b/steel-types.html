Steel Types Used in Thermoset Molds

PLENCO’s years of experience in the thermoset molding industry have given us some insight into what steel to use in various components of the mold and press.

We offer to share these tips with our molding compound customers, for whatever value they may have. As with all suggestions, they may not necessarily apply in every circumstance, but we believe that they are generally accurate and we hope you find them helpful. (Steel Types Used in Thermoset Molds text)
