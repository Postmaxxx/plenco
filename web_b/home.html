Plenco Products

Phenolic Novolac and Resol Resins

Our extensive range of phenolic resins are used in applications to impart a desirable combination of high hardness, long-term thermal and mechanical stability, high fire and flame resistance, combined with good electrical insulating capability.

resins

Phenolic Molding Compounds

Highly engineered and custom formulated thermosets are used across a number of industries including automotive, aerospace, industrial, electrical and more. See how you can benefit from our composites.

phenolic

BMC/Thermoset Polyester Molding Compounds

Bulk Molding Compounds are used for high-strength applications that can be evaluated to replace steel, die cast aluminum or iron when reduction of weight and secondary operation c osts are important goals.

BMC/ THERMOSET

Melamine-Phenolic Molding Compounds

Our melamine-phenolic (MP) compounds add strength and electrical insulating characteristics to many applications. MP molding compounds feature enhanced tracking resistance, arc resistance and surface abrasion during repetitive cycling.
