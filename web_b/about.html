About Plenco

Infinite chemistry

For over 85 years Plastics Engineering Company has been developing new phenolic chemistries and performance materials. These advanced materials connect our world together to create a safer and cleaner world in which we live. Come and benefit from the Plenco difference.

Quality Assurance/Philosophy

Plenco’s mission is to respond to our customers’ needs by producing high-quality, defect-free materials. This has been a cornerstone of the company’s philosophy since it was founded in 1934. To accomplish this we strive to establish solid relationships with our customers, recognizing that the quality of the products and services we provide is an integral part of our customers’ success. Trained quality assurance personnel, following globally recognized test methods, provide a high level of confidence in the uniform quality of our products. State-of-the-art test equipment, supported by a statistically oriented, computer-based calibration program, assures accurate, consistent, and reliable test data and process control.

Quality plans are developed with input solicited from our customers and interdepartmental teams at Plenco. In addition to the general certification assured by the Plenco trademark, products can be certified to a number of standards including ISO, UL, JIS, CSA, and DOD. (Quality Assurance text)

Certifications

Plenco believes the quality of its products is second to none, but more importantly that the search for quality improvement never ends.

Formal corrective and preventive action procedures drive our continuous improvement program. These procedures have kept Plenco products in step with the increasing expectations of our customers in today’s market where consistent quality is a key ingredient of success. (Quality Assurance text)

Customer Relations

At Plenco we are committed to building strategic relationships with our customers. To achieve this goal, Plenco is dedicated to the following principles of doing business: Consistently manufacturing quality molding materials and resins. (Customer Relations text)

  • Meeting or exceeding customers’ total expectations.
  • Participating in an exchange of knowledge and ideas to enhance our customers’ processing capabilities.
  • Meeting periodically with our customers’ management, technical, and manufacturing personnel to review continuous improvement toward mutual goals.
  • Constantly exploring options for cost containment. (Customer Relations text)
  • Maximizing responsiveness to our customers through lead-time reduction and optimum delivery performance.
  • Optimizing material handling efficiencies with EDI, bar coding, and other electronic systems.
    (Customer Relations text)
  • Regularly exploring continuous improvements in processing techniques.
  • Providing prompt and thorough engineering support and technical service.
  • Continually improving all levels of the supplier/customer relationship.
    (Customer Relations text)

Plenco’s History

Plastics Engineering Company, a family owned and managed business founded in 1934, established as its corporate mission a sincere desire to respond efficiently to the needs of our customers through development, manufacture, and servicing of useful, high-value products.

Our mission is accomplished by an uncompromising adherence to customer requirements and safety in manufacturing, with consideration given to our environment and surrounding community, and with sensitivity to the needs of our employees. (Company Information text)
